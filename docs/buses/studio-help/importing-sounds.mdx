![Instructional Pic](https://cdn.discordapp.com/attachments/1109301713464205315/1116174102286315530/image.png)

**Import the sound to ROBLOX** [here](https://create.roblox.com/dashboard/creations?activeTab=Audio)

![Instructional Pic](https://cdn.discordapp.com/attachments/1109301713464205315/1116175538160144414/image.png)

**When your sound is imported to ROBLOX, click the 3 dots and click *Copy Asset ID***

![Instructional Pic](https://cdn.discordapp.com/attachments/1109301713464205315/1116175829387448330/image.png)

**In ROBLOX Studio, find the sound and replace the *SoundId* with the ID you recently copied**

![Instructional Pic](https://cdn.discordapp.com/attachments/1109301713464205315/1116176246158671932/image.png)


**For granting other games/experiences access to use these sounds,** [visit this ROBLOX Forums site](https://create.roblox.com/docs/sound/assets#granting-permissions)
